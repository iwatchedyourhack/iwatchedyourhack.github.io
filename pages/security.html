Reporting security issues

We know security is important. Mistakes are for people, and no single person can think of, and fix, everything.

That's why we would appreciate it if you would report any vulnerabilities found in this page, system or host to us. If it's not critical please email us at admin@iwatchedyourhack.com and give as much details as you can. Also, you can send us a DM on twitter at @0xIWYH for better and encrypted contact details.

We will publicly thank you for your report after the issue has been fixed, and your name will be added to the special thanks section at the bottom of this page.
